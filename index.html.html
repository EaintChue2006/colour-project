What Is Color Thephy?

Color therapy,also known as chromotherapy, is a form of therapy that uses color and loght to treat certain mental and physical health conditions.We can trace this form of therapy back to the ancient Egyptians.They made use of sun-filled rooms with colored glasses for therapeutic purposes

  • Red: Red is used to energize or invigorate a person who might be feeling tired or down.However,red may also trigger who might already be tense,which is why it's used carefully in color therapy.

  • Green: Green is the color of nature,and according to chromatherapists,it can help rebeve stress and relax a person.
